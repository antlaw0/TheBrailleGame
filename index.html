One Minute Madness- Braille


In this game, you have 60 seconds to use the keyboard as a braille keyboard to enter the corresponding letter that is spoken and shown on screen as many times as you can within the time limit. Use the F, D, S and J, K, L keys as if they were the buttons on a Braille keyboard. For example, F is for dot 1, D is for dot 2, S is for dot 3, J is for dot 4, K is for dot 5, and L is for dot 6.
