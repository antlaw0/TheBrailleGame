One Minute Madness- Braille


In this game, you have 60 seconds to use the keyboard as a braille keyboard to enter the corresponding letter that is spoken and shown on screen as many times as you can within the time limit. Use the F, D, S and J, K, L keys as if they where the buttons on a Braille keyboard.
  • F is dot 1
  • D is dot 2
  • S is dot 3
  • J is dot 4
  • K is dot 5
  • L is dot 6
